I am a Research Software Engineer at Google Research working with Dr. Aravindan Raghuveer on privacy preserving deep learning. Before this, I was a Pre-Doctoral Researcher at Google Research, where I worked with Dr. Pradeep Shenoy on robust and interpretable deep learning.

In the past, I've spent two wonderful summers with Abhirut Gupta at IBM Research India and Debdoot Mukherjee at ShareChat working on NLP and creative text generation. I've also worked on multimodal representation learning for my Masters thesis, advised by Dr. Pramod Singh and Atri Mandal at IBM Research India.

I graduated with a B.Tech and M.Tech in Information Technology from ABV-IIITM, Gwalior, India in 2020.
